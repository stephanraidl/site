Personalised progression

1-on-1
Coaching

Your ride. Your pace.
My full attention.

Wingfoil progression isn't linear — it's about trusting your skill and building the right muscle memory. In a private session, we zero in on exactly what's holding you back and build a clear path forward.

Sessions run 2–3 hours on the water, with video analysis, debrief, and a personalised drills plan to take away. Available year-round in person, get in touch to check availabiltiy.

Who it's for

All levels, real results, own equipment required

01

Complete Beginner

Never stood on a foil before? We start from the ground up — wing handling, board control, and that first flight moment you'll never forget.

02

Progressing Rider

You're riding but want to go upwind reliably, link jibes and tacks, or get more comfortable in chop. We'll diagnose and keep you flying.

03

Advanced

Wave riding, jumping, freestyle tricks, or just becoming more comfortable in all conditions. Push your ceiling with high-level feedback.
