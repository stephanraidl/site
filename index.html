About

Born for the wind.
Built for the foil.

I've spent the last years chasing winds windows in the northern and southern atlantic, learning the rhythm of the sea and the language of the waves.

Winging in the Netherlands, Germany, Denmark to Italy, Madeira, Canary Islands and Cape Verde, every destination has it's magic

Proudly sponsored by Armstrong, I am based out of the Netherlands raw, windswept northwest coast — I coach this summer again with Dirk Herpel, Chief Editor of the wingsurfers magazine in one of the best wave spots in Europe.

Whether you're a beginner looking to improve your skills, or an experienced rider looking to push into waves and big air, we will meet you exactly where you are.
