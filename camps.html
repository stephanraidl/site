A week that changes everything

Each camp is designed as a complete experience — great food and stay at the stormfarm, with breakfast, dinner and sauna.

Located in Klitmøller we are close to the spot a 15 minutes away from Hanstholm and Voropør so plenty to choose from. In case the wind direction doesn't work we have a lake, and a fjord where we can do downwinders.

The coaching sessions will be in the morning and afternoon about 2h each. We have plenty of gear and are 2 instructors so we can split up into two groups for different levels. Maximum 10 riders per camp, so everyone gets real attention on the water. We have cameras and a professional fotografer and will do analysis on and off the water, explaining the spots and coach you at your level.

Klitmøller — Cold Hawaii — is one of the best if not the best northern European wave spots, honouring its name.

Every camp includes:

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need my own gear?

Yes you should bring your own setup so you have what you are used to ride. We do have other boards, wings and foils you can try out, but the easiest and best gear is always the gear you know best.

How do I get to Cold Hawaii?

Drive by car, it's about 3–4 hours from Flensburg in Germany. Or fly into Billund (BLL) or Aalborg (AAL), then rent a car for the ~1.5–2h drive to Klitmøller. I can help coordinate arrivals.

What level do I need to be?

You should be able to stand on your board and be able to hold the wing in your hands without falling into the water. It would be beneficial if you have foiled your first meters — jibes and tacks not required but good to have ;-)

What if the wind doesn't show up?

Cold Hawaii is one of the windiest spots in Europe — it almost always delivers. On light-wind days we do theory, video review, dry land drills, or explore the local area. You'll never be bored.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely — most people do. The small group format means you'll quickly feel at home.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 60 days before the camp. 50% refund between 30–60 days. Within 30 days, the spot can be transferred to another person. Get in touch and we'll work it out.
