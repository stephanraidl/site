An athlete who actually uses the gear.

Wingfoil is still a sport where authenticity wins. My audience follows because they see real sessions, real progression, and real honest reviews — not polished ads. I only work with brands I genuinely use and believe in.

Based in the Netherlands close to Scheveningen and travelling to iconic places like el Médano, Tarifa, Klitmøller, Hanstholm, Ponta Preta amongst others — I spend many days on the water and share the stoke and knowledge with the community.

My fast progression has made the collaboration as a team rider with Armstrong a very fruitful one.

Teaching privately and for Wingcollege in the past, as well as having represented Armstrong at Boot Düsseldorf and the Foil Festival on Fehmarn — I am an active and unique individual in the scene.

Collaboration with Wingsurfers Magazine for the Cold Hawaii camps connects me deeply with the German wingfoil scene.
